Help/FAQ

Common Issues

  • Issue: Unable to schedule an appointment.
    Solution: Ensure all fields are filled out correctly, including the date, time, and customer name. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or contact support.
  • Issue: Technician schedule not displaying.
    Solution: Clear your browser cache or try accessing the schedule from a different browser. If the issue continues, please reach out to IT support.
  • Issue: Service plan purchase not processing.
    Solution: Check that your payment information is correct. You can also try switching between different payment methods or contact customer service for further assistance.

User Guide

Welcome to the Appliance Warehouse SIM System User Guide. Below are the instructions to help you navigate and use the system:

  1. Logging In: Use your assigned credentials to log in to the system. If you have issues logging in, please reset your password or contact your system administrator.
  2. Scheduling Appointments: Navigate to the "Appointment Management" section, select "Schedule Appointment," and fill in the required fields such as date, time, and customer details. Once done, click "Schedule Appointment."
  3. Viewing Appointments: To view scheduled appointments, navigate to "Appointment Management" and select "View Appointments." You will see a list of all the upcoming appointments.
  4. Technician Portal: Access technician schedules, update work status, or request parts under the "Technician Portal" section. All necessary forms and options will be available there.
  5. Service Plans: You can view or purchase service plans in the "Service Plans" section. Choose the plan that best suits your needs, fill out the required information, and complete the purchase process.
  6. Reports: Access detailed, summary, or exception reports in the "Reports" section to analyze service usage, parts usage, or appointment summaries.
  7. Customer Information: Search for customer details and view their service history in the "Customer Information" section.
  8. Help/FAQ: If you need assistance, visit the "Help/FAQ" section where you can find common issues and solutions, or access this user guide.
